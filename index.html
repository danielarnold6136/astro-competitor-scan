§02

Stages to clear before launch

One stage at a time. Nothing in a later stage starts until the one before it is signed off.

  1. Stage 1 — Deep competitor researchrunning now
    28 competitor dossiers + 6 full funnel walks + 10 cross-cutting studies + adversarial verification, compressed into FOUNDATION.md. This is the evidence base everything else rests on. Deliverable: the foundation document. Nothing gets built until this is read and signed off.
  2. Stage 2 — Offer & funnel specafter S1 sign-off
    Lock the exact ladder (trial price → $14.99/wk → $39.99/mo), the screen-by-screen quiz spec with real question wording, the reveal choreography and the paywall layout. Deliverable: a spec precise enough to build from without further decisions.
  3. Stage 3 — Tech buildthe long pole
    Next.js funnel, quiz state, birth-data capture with lat/lon/timezone geocoding, chart-engine integration, the Claude reading generator, Stripe subscription + trial, webhooks for renewal and dunning, analytics. Deliverable: funnel live to paywall on a staging domain.
  4. Stage 4 — Creative & ad opsparallel with S3
    First creative batch off the ranked hook library, Business Manager / Page-fleet structure, pixel + CAPI, domain verification, warm-up sequence. Deliverable: ad account ready and creatives approved. This is the stage the VA can own most of.
  5. Stage 5 — Email & retentionbefore first spend
    ESP wired, SPF/DKIM/DMARC passing, welcome + reading-delivery + trial-conversion + pre-rebill + dunning + win-back sequences written. Deliverable: every automated email live and tested. Must exist before traffic, not after.
  6. Stage 6 — Legal & billing hardeninggate before spend
    Disclaimer wording, auto-renew disclosure that satisfies ROSCA / California ARL, one-click cancel, refund policy, terms/privacy, Stripe descriptor and dispute tooling. Deliverable: a billing setup that will not get the Stripe account frozen. Hard gate — no traffic until this passes.
  7. Stage 7 — Soft launch, one geothe 2-week target
    Small daily budget, one geo, one hook, real traffic. Watch quiz completion, email capture, paywall view, trial start, then trial→paid. Deliverable: the first real conversion data. Not scale — signal.
  8. Stage 8 — Read the data, then scaleafter signal
    Fix the worst drop-off, add the second hook and the second geo, then the upsell ladder and the palmistry add-on if Stage 1 says it earns its friction. Deliverable: a decision to scale, kill or pivot — made on numbers.
